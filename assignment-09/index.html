Pumpkin Bars

Ingredients

    { background-color: lightgrey; }
  • 4 large eggs, room tempature
  • 1 2/3 cups sugar
  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 1 can solid-packed Pumpkin
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoonsbaking powder
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 or 2 tablespoons whole milk

      • Icing Ingredients

        { background-color: lightgrey; }
      • 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
      • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
      • 1/4 cup butter, softened
      • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      • 1 or 2 tablespoons whole milk

      Directions

        { background-color: lightgrey; } In a bowl, beat the eggs, sugar, oil and pumpkin until well blended. Combine the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda and salt; gradually add to pumpkin mixture and mix well. Pour into an ungreased 15x10x1-in. baking pan. Baske at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until set. Cool completely.
      1. For icing,beat the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, butter and vanilla in a small bowl. Add enough milk to acieve spreading consistency. Spread over bars. Store in the refrigerator.
