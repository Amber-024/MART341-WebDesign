Approximately 5 years later he joined The New Yorker Magazine and began to wrtie books. He wrote over seventeen books and was wlwcted the American Academy of Arts and Letters in 1973. Although he is most know for children's books,such as Charlotte's Web and Stuart Little, he also wrote adult books as well.
