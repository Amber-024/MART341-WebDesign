Tatertot Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 small onion
  • 16 oz package of frozen tatertots
  • 1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
  • 2/3 cups of milk or water
  • 1 cup chredded cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

      • Directions

      1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large skillet cook beef and onion over a medium heat until the meat is fully cooked, and then drain. Season with salt and pepper.
      2. Transfer meat and onion to a 2-qt baking dish. Top with Tatertots. Combine soup and milk, and then pour over the tatertots and meat and sprinkle the top with cheese.
      3. Bake uncovered for 30-40 minutes or until mixture is heated all the way through.
